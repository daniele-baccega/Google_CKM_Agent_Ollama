### **Key Recommendations**

#### **1. SGLT2i (Empagliflozin) Management**
- **Hold empagliflozin 24–48 hours pre-op** (per **AHA/ACC 2024 Perioperative Guidelines** for non-cardiac surgery in CKD/HFrEF).
  - *Rationale*: Risk of **hypotension** (synergistic with ACEi) and **acute GFR decline** intraoperatively. Resume **post-op Day 1–2** if:
    - **BP stable** (≥100 mmHg systolic, no orthostatic changes).
    - **No signs of dehydration/hypovolemia** (e.g., tachycardia, oliguria).
    - **eGFR ≥30 mL/min/1.73m²** (monitor post-op Day 1).
  - *Alternative*: If surgery is delayed >48 hours, consider **holding until post-op** to mitigate GFR dip.

#### **2. ACEi (Lisinopril) Management**
- **Hold lisinopril 12–24 hours pre-op** (per **KDIGO/AHA guidelines** for peri-operative CKD).
  - *Rationale*: Risk of **intra-operative hypotension** (especially with HFrEF) and **post-op AKI** due to volume shifts.
  - **Resume post-op Day 1** if:
    - **BP stable** (≥110 mmHg systolic, no orthostatic hypotension).
    - **No AKI** (eGFR ≥ baseline or <25% decline from pre-op).
    - **No signs of HF decompensation** (e.g., elevated JVP, pulmonary edema).
  - *Monitor*: Serial **eGFR/Cr** on post-op Days 1–3.

#### **3. Metformin Management**
- **Hold metformin 48 hours pre-op** (per **ADA guidelines** for CKD Stage 3b + surgery).
  - *Rationale*: Risk of **lactic acidosis** with:
    - **Hypoperfusion** (peri-operative hypotension).
    - **Contrast** (if inadvertently administered).
    - **Dehydration** (common post-op).
  - **Resume post-op** if:
    - **eGFR ≥45 mL/min/1.73m²** (check post-op Day 1).
    - **No AKI** (stable Cr/eGFR).
    - **No metabolic acidosis** (lactate levels if clinically indicated).

#### **4. Beta-Blocker (Carvedilol)**

  - *Dose*: **Reduce to 50% of maintenance dose** if:
    - **HR <60 bpm** or **BP <100 mmHg systolic**.
    - **Hypotension risk** (e.g., pre-op fasting, dehydration).
  - *Resume full dose post-op* if:
    - **HR ≥60 bpm** and **BP stable**.
    - **No signs of HF decompensation**.

#### **5. Peri-Operative Fluid/Electrolyte Management**
- **Intra-operative**:
  - **Goal**: **Euvolemia** (avoid fluid overload in HFrEF).
